professional-level skills for junior analyst roles in Geneva trading houses 
(Trafigura, Glencore, Traxys).

## Focus Areas
- Rare earths & critical minerals
- Energy commodities (LNG, crude oil)
- Financial derivatives: futures, swaps, options
- Market analysis & geopolitical risk
- Python for data analysis and backtesting
